About Me

K. Marie Brown

Hi, I'm Kaphillis Marie Brown and I'm a UX Designer.

I gained many transferable skills in my previous Legal and Contracting administrative positions. After a few years of using the left side of my brain, I decided it was time for a career change.

I completed an A.A.S. in Digital Media to gain programming and design skills. Now that I'm using my creative side, I feel like I've become unleashed. Seriously, who doesn't love seeing their ideas come to life online?

In my free time I enjoy spending time with family, bike riding, and spending time in nature. I love listening to music in any format and karaoke is my absolute favorite thing to do.

I am originally from the far north suburbs of Chicago. However, the wonderful life as a military wife relocated our family to San Antonio, TX. We now call it home.
